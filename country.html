What does it do?

Among one of the poorest countries of the world, the Comoros does nonetheless have a large export and farming industry, among other things.

Exports

The Comoros' main economic power comes from exports.
Their main exports are vanilla and cloves, with some other spices thrown in as well. They are the
tenth-largest exporter of vanilla in the world, impressive for their size.

History

The Comoros were originally a Portuguese settlement, due to the Portuguese base in Madagascar. However, the French took it over and still hold a portion of the archipelago: the island of Mayotte.
